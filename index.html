03.

How It
Works

Encrypt

Drop a file. AES-256-GCM encryption runs instantly in your browser with a random 256-bit key. The ciphertext leaves your machine — the key does not.

Upload

The encrypted blob is registered on Aptos blockchain and distributed across Shelby storage providers. No central server. Redundant by default.

Share

Copy the link. It contains the blob hash and decryption key in the URL fragment. Recipients download from Shelby nodes and decrypt locally. No trust needed.

04.

FAQ

Can Blobbed read my files?

No. Encryption happens client-side before upload. We never see the key. We never see the plaintext.

What if Blobbed shuts down?

Your files live on Shelby Protocol nodes, not our servers. The link still works even if this domain goes offline.

Why does upload require a wallet?

Registering a blob requires an on-chain transaction on Aptos. Downloading is free and requires no wallet.
